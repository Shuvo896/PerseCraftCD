Quick Format Guide:
• LHS: Single uppercase letter (A-Z)
• Epsilon: eps or ϵ
• Terminals: lowercase (a,b,id,+)
• Non-terminals: uppercase (A-Z)

Mode-Specific Rules:
Left Recursion: No spaces → E→E+T|T
Left Factoring & FIRST/FOLLOW: Spaces OK → E → E + T | T

Real Examples:
E→E+T|T (Left Recursion)
A→aB|aC|b (Left Factoring)
S→AB|C (FIRST/FOLLOW)

End input with: Empty line or END
