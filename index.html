Input grammar rules one per line.
Use format: A->aB|c (no spaces around -> or |) for Left Recursion mode.
For Left Factoring and First & Follow modes, spaces around -> and | are allowed.
LHS must be a single uppercase letter (A-Z). Use eps for epsilon (empty string).
Example:
S->Sa|b (Left Recursion)
A -> aB | aC | b (Left Factoring)
S -> A B | C (First & Follow)
