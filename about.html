About PerseCraft

Grammar tooling crafted for compiler enthusiasts

PerseCraft is a lightweight web-based toolkit built to help students and developers experiment with context-free grammars in a fast and interactive way. It focuses on essential compiler design operations such as left recursion elimination, left factoring, and FIRST & FOLLOW set computation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make core compiler design concepts more approachable by turning abstract grammar transformations into something you can see, tweak, and understand in real time. The goal is to reduce friction for learners while still staying close to the theory used in real-world parser construction.

Our Vision

PerseCraft aims to grow into a companion tool for courses, workshops, and self-learners who want to build a stronger intuition for how parsers work under the hood. Over time, we plan to extend support for more analyses, visualizations, and export options that integrate smoothly into academic and hobby projects.
